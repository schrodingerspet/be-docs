# Algorithms: Temporal Correlation vs Shallow Models

Understand why temporal models (1D-CNN) beat shallow models (SVM) for False Data Injection attacks.

### LSTM & 1D-CNN Performance
The 1D-CNN model achieved the highest performance (**96.13% F1-score**) against complex attacks by exploiting temporal correlations across the data fusion stream. 

<Callout type="info">
While SVMs (Support Vector Machines) and FNNs (Feed-Forward Neural Networks) treat each row of telemetry independently, Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) and 1D-CNN models evaluate multiple timesteps to understand the *trajectory* of the drone's behavior.
</Callout>

### Results
Cyber-physical models outperformed cyber-only and physical-only models by 1-4% in F1-scores. Fusion prevents overfitting while achieving faster convergence.

- **Cyber-only:** Vulnerable to spoofed data packets.
- **Physical-only:** Delayed reaction times to early-stage payload injection.
- **Fused:** The 1D-CNN catches the dissonance between the spoofed packet and the physical drift.
